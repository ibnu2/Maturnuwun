montair

MONTAIR offers a range of spesifically-designed products to fulfil the various types of applications, based on total climate control, reliability and energy effeciency, in order to optimize the investment made in the system.

advantage using montair product


TOTAL CLIMATE CONTROL


LONGER SYSTEM SERVICE LIFE

MONTAIR units are designed especially for rooms with a specific high thermal load and ensure excellent thermal-hygrometric levels.

  • CONSTANT TEMPERATURE. The use of inverter * compressors prevents fluctuations in temperature, which could otherwise be damaging to servers.
  • LOW HUMIDITY, HIGH SHR. Extremely high Sensible Capacity levels, up to 100% SHR : accurate dehumidification prevents the risk of condensation and corrosion on the racks, while maintaining levels to prevent static loads, harmful to data security.
    Dedicated versions equipped with humidifying and/or dehumidifying systems maintaining the optimal level of humidity in every situation.
  • FILTERING SYSTEM, CLEAN AIR. The air is kept free of impurities that can damage racks thanks to dedicated filtering system in latex and high filtration fibre (standard M5, accessory F7-EU7).

    • LONG-TERM RELIABILITY


    SHUT-DOWN PREVENTION : THE DATA IS PROTECTED

    MONTAIR precission air conditioners, designed to operate 24/7, 365 day a year, feature components specifically designed for continuous use (heavy duty) and maximum security. The materials are among the most resistant on the market, such as the paints and panels with thermo-acoustic and fireproof insulation that the unit structure is made of.
    Specific models are designed according to an internal redudancy system to ensure System operation even in the case of unit failure, such as the dual fluid and dual coil models, or the double coiling circuit versions. The LOGICA rear cooling door System is a patented system against hydraulic leaks to guarantee maximum data protection.
    The production plan is closely monitored throughout every phase of production, up to final testing of the units, in terms of quality and performance. In the after-sales phase, the Technical Assistance Centres distributed extensively throughout the international territory provide, on request, the on-site unit start-up in addition to complete technical assistance, quickly and precisely, during unit service life.

    MAXIMUM ENERGY EFFECIENCY THROUGHOUT THE ENTIRE YEAR'S CYCLE


    REDUCED OPERATING COSTS

    In Data Centre Systems, a considerable part of total consumption is due to air conditioning on year -round operation. Accordingly, it is essential to design a cooling system that minimises energy consumption

  • INVERTER TECHNOLOGY. The high-effeciency line for medium-desity systems with Scroll Inverter compressors and EC Inverter Plug-Fans features up to 50% less consumption at partial loads in comparison to traditional solutions.
  • LOGICA SYSTEM. The integrated cooling system for high destiny systems that offers up to 93% in energy saving in comparison to traditional solutions.
  • FREE-COOLING. Dedicated MONTAIR cooling systems are designed with the Indirect Free-Cooling logic, others can also be connected to external liquid Chillers equipped with Free-Cooling technology.
