Telefire

Telefire's 7000 series includes addressable analog control units for fire detection and extingushing. Telefire's 7000 series is a global leading and consistently maintains a high standard and a reliable reputation.

The systems are designed and manufactured strictly according to US and European standard; they control and offer solutions for the most recent requirements in modern building, including built-in smoke management, automatic extinguishing, addressable PA, firemen's telephone, management control and integration with other system.

The 7000 series control units manage addressable input devices such as: smoke detectors, heat detectors, combined detectors, alarm buttons, etc.

The control units are based on advanced unique technology and quality components, which provide a maximal reliable solutions for various needs and requirements of fire detection and extinguishing.

The 7000 series systems are designed as a modular environment, open systems with flexible interface allowing quick and easy integration ot external devices, in instalation, maintenance, control and reporting subsystem.

    Series 7000
    Alternative Control

    GUARD - 7

    An addressable analog smart control unit that can control up to 60 devices.

    The GUARD-7 is designed for relatively small sites, with the advanced features and capabilities of large site.

    SAVER - 7000

    An analog smart control unit that can control a single loop of 127 addresses, and can be expanded by an additional loop to allow the ability of controlling up to 254 addresses.

    The SAVER-7000 is designed for small to medium sites that require the functions of fire detection, automatic extinguishing, management and control such as smoke management, addressable public address and firemen's telephone.

    ADR - 7000

    An analog smart control unit developed using new and advanced technology.

    The ADR-7000 is suitable for installation in large and complex projects in which an advanced system is required with capabilities for complex control, management and programming.

    It is a modular system that can be expanded to up to 8 loops and up to a total of 1016 smart devices in a single control unit.

    The control unit can be connected by peer-to-peer wiring in a control unit network.
